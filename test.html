Welcome to Seltzer Film & Video

Providing professional quality, affordable video solutions that help your cause, non-profit, or business get noticed. Telling your story through powerful video that brings your vision and message to life.

We work closely with you to create high-impact videos that reach your audience.

Our team helps nonprofits and businesses with cost effective budgets to corporations producing high-end campaigns. Seltzer Film & Video, LLC has produced hundreds of videos for award-winning television programs and websites for over 15 years.

Contact us about producing videos that get the word out about your nonprofit, business or cause.

What We Do

Seltzer Film & Videos seasoned professional team creates your video with the latest state of the art camera equipment and editing software.
We strive to get your message heard. If its important to you, its important to us!

Nonprofit Videos

We specialize in producing thought provoking videos for nonprofits and causes that inspire change. We can expand your reach by giving a powerful voice to your message through video.

Documentaries

Our documentary films shed light on environmental causes, biographical stories, social justice issues and can give voice to those may not have the opportunity to be heard.

Marketing Videos

Our videos build your brand awareness and raise your credibility and trust. We can help your business or nonprofit grow with well crafted videos for use on your website and social media platforms, that appeal to your target audience.

Legal Videos
